Steam

I made my Steam account in 2011, specifically to play Killing Floor with my friends. Before that, I had no idea something like Steam could exist. I was still living in the world of buying PC games at the store, especially Blizzard games. Little did I know that Steam would become the primary way I buy and play video games ever since. A big thank you to Valve for supporting Linux gaming.
